Privacy Policy

Fakelit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our hosting, domain, web app development, and payment processing services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide when registering or using our services (e.g., name, email, payment details).
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To process payments and manage your account.
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and support.
  • To improve our services and ensure security.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share information with trusted partners for payment processing, hosting, or legal compliance.

4. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related requests or questions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
