Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What hosting packages do you offer?

We offer three main hosting packages: Starter ($99/month for up to 10 sites), Professional ($199/month for up to 50 sites), and Enterprise ($399/month for up to 150 sites). All packages include auto-scaling, 99.9% uptime guarantee, and 24/7 support.

How quickly can you set up payment processing?

Payment processing setup typically takes 1-3 business days. We offer both NMI and Stripe integration with setup fees starting at $250 and monthly fees of $25 plus transaction fees.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 technical support through email, phone, and live chat. Our support team is available around the clock to assist with any issues or questions.

What is your response time for support requests?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent issues, our phone support is available 24/7 for immediate assistance.
