About Michael Rodriguez

Michael Rodriguez is a certified Substance Abuse Counselor with over 15 years of experience supporting individuals in recovery. He specializes in harm reduction strategies, relapse prevention, and providing compassionate support for those struggling with substance use disorders.

Areas of Expertise

  • Substance use disorder treatment
  • Harm reduction strategies
  • Relapse prevention planning
  • Recovery support and motivation
  • Family support and education
  • 12-step and alternative recovery approaches

Approach

Michael uses a non-judgmental, person-centered approach that meets individuals where they are in their recovery journey. He combines evidence-based treatment methods with practical support to help clients build sustainable recovery and improve their quality of life.

How I Can Help You

Recovery Support

Harm Reduction
