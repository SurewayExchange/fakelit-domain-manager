About Lisa Thompson

Lisa Thompson is our dedicated Onboarding Specialist who welcomes new users to CareConnect and guides them through their mental health journey. With over 8 years of experience in user experience and mental health advocacy, Lisa helps individuals find the right counselor and services for their needs.

Areas of Expertise

  • Platform orientation and navigation
  • Counselor matching and recommendations
  • Service explanation and guidance
  • Resource connection and referrals
  • User support and troubleshooting
  • Mental health education and awareness

Approach

Lisa takes a warm, welcoming approach to help new users feel comfortable and confident using CareConnect. She listens to your needs, explains available services, and connects you with the most appropriate counselor for your situation.
