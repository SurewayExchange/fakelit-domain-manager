About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a licensed Clinical Psychologist with over 20 years of experience in cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT). She specializes in treating anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders using evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

Areas of Expertise

  • Anxiety disorders and panic attacks
  • Depression and mood disorders
  • Stress management and burnout
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness and relaxation techniques
  • Trauma-informed care

Approach

Dr. Mitchell uses a compassionate, evidence-based approach combining CBT techniques with mindfulness practices. She helps clients identify and change negative thought patterns while developing healthy coping strategies for long-term mental wellness.

How I Can Help You

Cognitive Therapy

Identify and challenge negative thought patterns that contribute to anxiety and depression.

Emotional Support

Provide compassionate support while helping you develop healthy emotional regulation skills.

Coping Strategies

Learn practical techniques to manage stress, anxiety, and difficult emotions effectively.

Personal Growth

Work towards building resilience and developing a more positive outlook on life.
