About Dr. Emily Chen

Dr. Emily Chen is a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with over 15 years of experience helping couples and families navigate relationship challenges. She specializes in improving communication, resolving conflicts, and strengthening family bonds.

Areas of Expertise

  • Couples counseling and relationship therapy
  • Family communication and conflict resolution
  • Pre-marital counseling and relationship preparation
  • Parenting support and family dynamics
  • Blended family challenges
  • Intimacy and emotional connection

Approach

Dr. Chen uses evidence-based approaches including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Gottman Method Couples Therapy, and Family Systems Theory. She creates a safe, non-judgmental space where all family members feel heard and understood.

How I Can Help You

Relationship Issues

Work through communication problems, trust issues, and emotional disconnection in your relationships.

Family Dynamics

Improve family relationships, resolve conflicts, and create healthier family patterns.

Communication Skills

Learn effective communication techniques to express needs and listen with empathy.

Conflict Resolution

Develop skills to resolve disagreements constructively and find mutually beneficial solutions.
