About Dr. Maria Garcia

Dr. Maria Garcia is a licensed Crisis Intervention Specialist with over 18 years of experience in emergency mental health support. She specializes in providing immediate assistance during mental health crises and helping individuals develop safety plans.

Areas of Expertise

  • Crisis intervention and de-escalation
  • Safety planning and risk assessment
  • Emergency mental health support
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Referral to appropriate services
  • Post-crisis follow-up support

Approach

Dr. Garcia uses evidence-based crisis intervention techniques to provide immediate support and stabilization. She focuses on safety first, then works to connect individuals with appropriate ongoing care and resources.

How I Can Help You

Crisis Support

Provide immediate emotional support and help you navigate through difficult moments safely.

Safety Planning

Work together to create a personalized safety plan for managing crisis situations.

Resource Connection

Connect you with appropriate mental health services and community resources.

Ongoing Support

Provide follow-up support and help you transition to long-term care when ready.
