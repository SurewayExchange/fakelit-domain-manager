Safety Policy

Your safety and well-being are our top priority

Our Commitment to Safety

CareConnect AI is committed to providing a safe, supportive environment for mental health conversations. We understand the sensitive nature of mental health discussions and have implemented comprehensive safety measures to protect our users.

Crisis Intervention Protocol

If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, our AI counselors are programmed to:

  • Recognize crisis situations and respond appropriately
  • Provide immediate crisis resources and hotline numbers
  • Encourage professional help and emergency services when needed
  • Never provide medical advice or replace professional care

Professional Boundaries

Our AI counselors maintain professional boundaries and:

  • Do not provide medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Do not prescribe medications
  • Do not engage in personal relationships
  • Always recommend professional help for serious concerns

Data Privacy and Security

We protect your privacy through:

  • End-to-end encryption for all conversations
  • Secure data storage with industry-standard protocols
  • No sharing of personal information with third parties
  • User control over their data and conversation history

Limitations of AI Support

While our AI counselors provide valuable support, they have limitations:

  • Not a replacement for professional mental health care
  • Cannot provide emergency medical assistance
  • May not recognize all crisis situations
  • Should not be used for severe mental health conditions

When to Seek Professional Help

Please seek immediate professional help if you experience:

  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
  • Severe depression or anxiety
  • Psychotic symptoms or hallucinations
  • Substance abuse issues
  • Any other mental health crisis
